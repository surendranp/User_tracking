Welcome to Dhaya Industries

Dhaya Industries is a major company engaged in the manufacturing of wide range of construction products such as heavy duty drycast paver blocks, table made glossy paver blocks, hollow concrete blocks, and fly ash bricks. All products are manufactured using high grade & quality raw material with latest generation world class automatic block making machines by dedicated teams of experts.

Our Products are being extensively used in construction and landscaping of commercial complexes, shopping malls, housing colonies, gardens, sports stadiums, walking & jogging tracks, heavy duty roads, container freight stations, airports, seaports, petrol pumps, swimming pools etc.
